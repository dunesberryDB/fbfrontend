Sustainability

In addition to our goal of achieving excellence in our products and work, we at Al-Farabi believe that adopting sustainability solutions is part of our success and that preserving the planet and its resources is part of our responsibility towards future generations. We also aim to enhance the confidence of our customers, employees, stakeholders, and communities in us by adhering to the procedures and laws of safety, health, and preservation of the environment in a distinguished manner by following a policy of transparent disclosure and cooperation with global environmental initiatives.

Al-Farabi succeeded and committed to reducing the intensity of carbon dioxide emissions by 20% over a period of up to 10 years. Al-Farabi followed a strategic plan for the program, represented in operational goals, social responsibilities, and the health and safety of workers.

Al-Farabi also paid attention to various initiatives, the most important of which are:

  • CDP, Carbon Disclosure Project
  • GRI, Global Reporting Initiatives
  • GPCA Sustainable Reporting
  • ACI's Sustainability Metrics Program
