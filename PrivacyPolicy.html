PRIVACY POLICY

We are pleased that you are visiting this website (“Site”). This Privacy Policy is maintained by FARABI GROUP (Farabi Petrochemicals Company, its subsidiaries, and affiliate companies), located at Jubail Industrial City, Abu Ali Road, Jubail (Telephone: +966133565000 and fax +966133565010).

FARABI GROUP is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. FARABI GROUP will only collect and process personal data to the extent it is allowed to do so, and will process personal data in a lawful, fair, and transparent manner. This document explains what personal data we collect, for what purpose, to whom we disclose the data, and for how long we keep the data. Additionally, we describe your rights and how you can invoke them. For the purpose of this policy, "FARABI GROUP" means all FARABI business units and entities, as well as affiliates that are controlled by FARABI GROUP. The objective of this data protection policy is to provide a uniform minimum standard for FARABI GROUP with respect to its protection of personal data worldwide. FARABI GROUP recognizes that certain local laws may require stricter standards than those described in this policy, in which case the stricter standards prevail. Where local law provides a lower level of protection for personal data than the level established by this data protection policy, the latter prevails. This policy applies to any digital and non-digital means of collecting your personal data. When we collect your personal data, we will provide you with more details and explain the purpose of such processing.


WHAT PERSONAL DATA DO WE COLLECT AND PROCESS?


In order to provide certain services, products, and information to you, we may ask that you register and provide us with information about you and/or your company, such as contact information and identifying, authenticating, location, and/or professional data. FARABI GROUP processes your personal data when you have given consent, the processing is needed for the performance of a contract, when FARABI GROUP is legally obliged to do so, or when the processing is necessary for the legitimate interests of FARABI GROUP.

In addition, FARABI GROUP will process personal data for the purposes for which it is collected. We will not use your contact information for product or promotional advertising unless you have specifically requested to receive product information.

Please make sure that anyone whose confidential information you give us has given their consent and is aware of our data protection policy before you give it to us.

We use cookies to make this site easier and better to use, to tailor our products and services to your interests and needs, and for marketing purposes. Cookies are files or pieces of information that may be stored on your computer (or other devices) when you visit a site.


FOR WHAT PURPOSES DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL DATA?


We will process your data for the purposes for which it is collected, such as commercial processes (e.g., commercial contacts, billing or invoicing sales, product delivery, processing orders, making or receiving payments for goods and/or services, permissible marketing activities, purchasing materials, supplies, equipment, supplier due diligence, customer screening processes, licensing agreements, and non-disclosure agreements, etc.), monitoring and ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulatory requirements, communications, responding to and complying with requests, career opportunities within FARABI GROUP, and safety and security.


WHAT DO WE DO TO PROTECT YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

To safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure,unlawful processing,accidental loss or damage and unauthorized destruction,FARABI GROUP has implemented the necessery technological,physical and organization safeguards


  • Access control
  • Security awareness and training
  • Security breach procedures
  • Contingency/emergency planning
  • Device and media controls
  • Audit controls
  • Data Integrity Controls
  • Storage and Transmission Security
  • Secure disposal

FARABI GROUP will require third-party service providers, who process Personal Data on its behalf, to implement similar security and confidentiality measures.


TO WHOM DO WE DISCLOSE YOUR PERSONAL DATA AND WHY?


To fulfill the purposes above, FARABI GROUP may need to disclose, transfer, or otherwise share your personal data within FARABI GROUP or with a third-party service provider to whom we have outsourced the processing of personal data. When FARABI GROUP outsources processing activities, it will ensure that the service provider is bound to implement and maintain adequate data protection and security measures and will comply with applicable privacy laws.

In some instances, FARABI GROUP may share the personal data of persons residing in the European Union as described above with entities located in countries outside of the European Economic Area (so-called "third countries") where applicable laws may not offer the same level of data protection as in the European Union. In such cases, FARABI GROUP takes measures to ensure an appropriate level of data protection and security.


TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE


Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when You go offline, while Session Cookies are deleted as soon as You close your web browser.

HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR PERSONAL DATA?


We will only hold your personal data as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which the data is collected before making it non-identifiable (anonymous) or deleting it, as required by law, unless there is a contractual or legal requirement to retain data for a longer period (such as required by trade or tax regulations).


WHAT ARE YOUR RIGHTS?


FARABI GROUP is committed to transparently processing your data. Depending upon your jurisdiction, you may have the right to request access to your personal data, rectification/correction, erasure (subject to the above-mentioned limitations), and data portability. Under certain conditions, you may also object to the processing of your personal data or restrict the processing. If you have given consent for processing your personal data, you have the right to withdraw your consent.

If you wish to exercise your rights, please send your request by email to dataprivacy@farabipc.com.


HOW CAN YOU FILE A COMPLAINT?


If you have a complaint regarding the manner in which FARABI GROUP processes your personal data, or suspect that a personal data breach has occurred, please contact us at dataprivacy@farabipc.com and provide sufficient details for us to revert to you.


HOW CAN YOU CONTACT US?


Please contact us if you have any questions about our Data Protection Policy and/or the manner in which we process your personal data by sending an email to dataprotection@farabipc.com.

This Data Privacy Policy is subject to change. We advise you to check it regularly
