C10 Normal Paraffin (Farapar C10)

C10 paraffin is a clear liquid and is classified as a light-grade paraffin. Its low aromatic content makes it ideal as a feedstock for chlorination and as a special solvent for some coatings.

Applications
  • Low Aromatic solvent for special coatings
  • Feedstock for Chlorination
