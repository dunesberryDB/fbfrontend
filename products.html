Detergent Feedstock

Description

Linear Alkyl Benzene is a major surfactant intermediate used in household Detergents. Farabi’s Linear Alkyl Benzene is produced using the most advanced UOP DETAL Process. The Product is well recognized in the market for its superior quality and is currently supplied to major multinational Detergent manufactures across Global locations.

Applications

  • Household detergents
  • Laundry powders
  • Dishwashing Liquids
  • Liquid household cleaners
  • Mixed with other surfactants in soap for foaming
