Our Working Environment

Farabi offers an ideal work environment to career aspirants, who are committed to professionalism and confident to take up challenges, giving priority to Saudi nationals. It also offers a competitive salary structure, attractive benefit packages, a high level of job satisfaction, and encouragement for career growth and development.

Emloyment Application

The application process at Farabi will begin with the list of available opportunities listed in our Careers portal. If you find a vacancy that you are interested in and qualified for, click on it for a more exploration of the job description and the competencies required. Create your account to apply for our career opportunities. Complete the application form and submit your resume. A member of our talent acquisition team will review your application and get back to you if your qualification and experience align with our expectations.

Benefits

our Benefits

FARABI provides competitive salary and benefits packages in par with the market, in order to attract and retain potential talents in the organization. It includes competitive salary ranges, attractive allowances, comprehensive medical coverage, housing loan programs, annual performance-linked bonus and increments. Farabi management also recognizes high performance and extraordinary value additions from the employees and rewards them through various reward programs.

Learning

LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT

FARABI recognizes that the most important resource of the organization is its employees. It is committed to training and developing the entire workforce to enhance their knowledge and equip them with the necessary skills and competencies to explore their full potential. This will enable the organization to achieve its goals and objectives through well-trained and competent working teams. All types of learning and development programs in the organization are automated and integrated via the SAP SuccessFactors suite.

Farabi Petrochemicals Group, located in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) is a global major in Linear Alkyl Benzene (LAB) & N-Paraffin business.
