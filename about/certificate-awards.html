FARABI PETROCHEMICAL COMPANY

Farabi Petrochemicals has earned a distinguished reputation in the industry, consistently demonstrating their commitment to excellence through a myriad of certificates and awards. Our unwavering dedication to quality and innovation has been recognized with prestigious certifications such as ISO 9001 for their robust quality management systems and ISO 14001 for their outstanding environmental management practices. Additionally, Farabi has been honored with numerous industry accolades, including the "Best Petrochemical Company" award, acknowledging their exceptional contributions to the petrochemical sector. These certificates and awards stand as a testament to Farabi's relentless pursuit of excellence and their position as a leader in the petrochemical industry.
