FARABI PETROCHEMICALS WINS ROYAL COMMISSION AWARDS FOR “BEST ENVIRONMENTAL PERFORMER”

Al-Farabi Petrochemical Company recently won the Royal Commission for Jubail and Yanbu Award for the best environmental performance among the Jubail Primary Industries Company for third place in 2020 during the Environment Day ceremony on June 6, 2021, which was organized by the Royal Commission in Jubail.
